The Batman

I was very skepitcal about Robert Pattison playing Batman because of his role in the Twilight series. I didn't think he had what it took to accurately portray a character like Batman. He proved me so wrong with his performance. The Batman has become one of my favorite movies because of it's portrayal of Batman. I always thought Batman should be portrayed as a dark character because he lives in Gotham City. That is exactly what they did in this movie. I highly recommend this movie to anyone.
